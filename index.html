Essential questions

Understand the model before you build.

Do I need a broker license to start?+

Not necessarily. The available structure depends on the state. In California, a corporation can hold a broker license when a qualified licensed broker serves as its Designated Officer. I help build the appropriate path for your location.

Is this just a DBA under Brokerise?+

The California agent-owned corporation model is not a DBA layered over my company. Your corporation is the licensed brokerage entity. Other states may use a different structure, which I explain clearly before you commit.

What happens when I earn my broker license?+

You can step into the broker role yourself and replace me. The goal is to preserve your brand, operating history and client relationships - without an unnecessary restart.

How do commissions work?+

You keep 100% of your commission, less the agreed flat transaction fee. There is no percentage split with a national franchise.

Can I add agents or mortgage services?+

Yes, when the licensing structure and your operating capacity support it. I can also help qualified owners explore real estate and mortgage licensing under one broader business strategy.

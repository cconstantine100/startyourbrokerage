NETLIFY MANUAL DEPLOY

1. Unzip the package.
2. In Netlify, open the site mapped to StartYourBrokerage.com.
3. Open Deploys.
4. Drag the entire netlify-deploy folder into the manual deploy area.
5. Wait for Published, then open StartYourBrokerage.com.

The folder is ready as-is. index.html is at its root.
